01What cookies are

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site work properly, remember your choices, and — in some cases — help the owner understand how the site is being used. Most websites use them in some form.

02How we use them

We keep our use of cookies to a minimum. Our website uses cookies only to help it function correctly and, where you have agreed, to help us understand how visitors find and use the site so we can improve it. We do not use cookies to track you across other websites, and we do not sell any information collected through them.

04Your consent

Under UK law, we must obtain your consent before any non-essential cookies are placed on your device. Where our site uses such cookies, you will be asked to accept or decline them — usually through a banner when you first visit — and you can change your choice at any time.

If our website uses only strictly necessary cookies, a consent banner may not be required. Your website provider can confirm which applies to your site.

05Managing cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Most browsers let you block cookies or remove those already stored.
