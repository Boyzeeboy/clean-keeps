A quieter way to keep a home

Home care, looked after for you

For the homes we look after, cleanliness is simply the beginning. A fine home asks for attentiveness — a sense of how each room should feel, a memory for the small things, and the discretion to move through a household as though you were never there at all.

CleanKeeps was founded on a single conviction: that the best home care is personal. We care for a deliberately small number of homes, returning week after week, season after season, until the rhythm of your home becomes second nature to us.

So you never need to think about it.

Our philosophy

We believe fine home care should be measured in trust, not in hours.

We do not bill by the hour or chase the lowest price. Instead, we offer a simple monthly care plan — a single, considered arrangement that reflects the standard of care your home receives and the relationship we build around it.

It means no clock-watching, no surprises, and no sense that the care is rationed. Only the quiet confidence of knowing your home is in dependable hands, every week of the year.
